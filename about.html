Chronicles: Interlude+

Game Type: PVP, FUN

Rates: x50.000

ПРЕАМБУЛА:

The events take place on Dino Island, scattered with mysterious creatures and inexplicable phenomena, long before the era when it would become home to dinosaurs...

CONCEPT:

Adena is the main and only currency on the server.

The maximum additional stats with full equipment, ultimate enchantment, and all possible buffs provide a +100% increase over the base stats that a character has in standard Draconic/Dark Crystal sets and S-grade weapons.

The main location is Dino Island, but the entire open world of Lineage 2 with classic monsters and raid bosses is also available.

START/RESPAWN:

Heroes start at level 80 with the status of Noble and equipment equivalent to Draconic and Dark Crystal sets. Profession selection, subclass changes, and tattoo applications are immediately available from NPC managers.

The inventory contains some consumables and a small starting capital. Arrows for bows, Soulshots, and Spiritshots are unlimited. Tattoo application, consumables, and teleportation are free.

EQUIPMENT:

2 levels of Tattoo (max enhancement +10)

2 levels of Jewelry (max enhancement +16)

3 levels of Armor (max enhancement +16)

4 levels of Weapons (max enhancement +25)

GAME MECHANICS:

Farming Zone - Mobs for farming are scattered across the entire island. You can go to any location (for example, Ketra) to farm there, but note that mobs on the island have a higher Adena drop rate.

Runes - Premium Runes and Frenzy Runes drop from mobs with a 0.5–1% chance. The Premium Rune grants +33% Adena drop for 20 minutes. The Frenzy Rune multiplies your stats by 100 for 12 seconds. How to use them is up to you.

Champion Mob System - There are Red and Blue enhanced mobs. They drop equipment enchantments, Skill Point Books, and Premium Runes.

Life-Stone Insertion - The system of farming Life-Stones and augmentation has been removed. You can insert the desired Life-Stone at the Inspirer NPC for Adena.

Item Selling - You can sell your items for their full value. This is convenient if you decide to change your playstyle.

BUFFER: The server also features an autonomous buffer with preset buff sets for mages and warriors. You can also create up to three custom buff sets.
